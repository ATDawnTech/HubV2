# AT Dawn Technologies - Talent Hub Roadmap

## Overview
This document outlines the proposed enhancements for the AT Dawn Technologies Talent Hub platform to provide comprehensive HR and workforce management capabilities.

## Implemented Features
- ✅ Employee Management (Records, Skills & Certifications)
- ✅ Auth fixes with @atdawntech.com domain restriction
- ✅ Onboarding workflows
- ✅ Productivity management basics

## Proposed Future Enhancements

### 1. Onboarding & Asset Management
**Scope**: Complete IT asset lifecycle and application access management
- Device assignments (laptops, phones, peripherals) with serial tracking
- Software license allocation and compliance monitoring  
- Application access checklist with automated provisioning
- Asset return workflows for departing employees

### 2. Leave & Holiday Calendar
**Scope**: Regional leave management with approval workflows
- Multi-region holiday calendars (US/IN/VN/SGP)
- Leave accrual calculations by location and tenure
- Manager approval workflows with delegation support
- Team calendar view for resource planning

### 3. OKRs & Performance Management
**Scope**: Goal setting and performance review system
- Cascading OKRs from company to individual level
- Quarterly review cycles with progress tracking
- 360-degree feedback collection and aggregation
- Performance calibration sessions

### 4. Bench & Allocation Management
**Scope**: Resource optimization and project allocation
- Bench status tracking with skill availability
- Smart matching for project requirements
- Allocation pipeline with forecasting
- Utilization alerts and recommendations

### 5. Vendor & Contractor Management
**Scope**: External workforce and contract tracking
- Contractor onboarding and offboarding
- Contract renewal reminders and rate tracking
- Performance scorecards and vendor evaluation
- Compliance documentation management

### 6. Security & Compliance
**Scope**: Policy management and audit trail
- Policy acknowledgment tracking
- Enhanced background check workflows
- SOC2 artifact checklist and evidence collection
- Security training completion monitoring

### 7. Learning & Training
**Scope**: Professional development tracking
- Mandatory course assignments and tracking
- Skill-based learning path recommendations
- Certification exam scheduling and results
- Training budget allocation and approval

### 8. Organizational Chart
**Scope**: Visual hierarchy and reporting structure
- Interactive org chart from manager relationships
- Quick actions for team restructuring
- Succession planning indicators
- Team composition analytics

### 9. Notifications Center
**Scope**: Centralized workflow and task management
- Digest of pending approvals and tasks
- Expiring certification alerts
- Onboarding milestone notifications
- Customizable notification preferences

### 10. Integrations
**Scope**: Seamless connectivity with existing tools
- Slack notifications for HR workflows
- Jira integration for project/task synchronization
- Google Drive for document storage and retrieval
- SSO implementation for unified authentication

## Implementation Priority
1. **High**: Leave Management, Org Chart, Notifications Center
2. **Medium**: Learning & Training, Security & Compliance, Bench Management
3. **Low**: OKRs, Vendor Management, Advanced Integrations

## Success Metrics
- Reduced manual HR processes by 80%
- Improved employee onboarding time by 50%
- 95% compliance with policy acknowledgments
- Real-time visibility into resource allocation
